About ITech Computer Institute

Empowering individuals with technology education since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, ITech Computer Institute began with a simple mission: to make quality computer education accessible to everyone. What started as a small training center with just two instructors has now grown into a premier institute with multiple locations and thousands of successful graduates.

Our founder, Rajesh Kumar, recognized the growing need for digital skills in the job market and set out to create programs that would give students practical, job-ready skills. Today, we continue that tradition by constantly updating our curriculum to match industry needs.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, affordable computer education that empowers individuals to succeed in the digital economy.

Our Vision

To be the leading computer education provider, recognized for our innovative teaching methods and exceptional student outcomes.
